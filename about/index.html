About

I am a final year engineering student at Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani (BITS-Pilani) located in the beautiful state of Goa in India. I will graduate in May 2019 with a B.E. (Hons.) in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering. I am currently in my final semester and after graduating I hope to pursue a M.S. in Computer Engineering.

In my penultimate semester, I completed my undergraduate thesis at the University of New South Wales, Sydney, under the supervision of Prof. Salil Kanhere and Dr. Sarang C. Dhongdi. Together with Ali Dorri and team, I worked on securing the Internet of Things using an optimized blockchain. I was also a member of the Security Reading Group there. This was a great learning experience for me, which intensified my interest in network security and privacy.

My research interests are in Computer Networking and the Internet of Things (IoT). I am particularly interested in improving the security and privacy of these networks. I like to keep myself updated on the latest activities in these fields. During my thesis I also developed an interest in Blockchain and Cryptography applications. I enjoy working with both hardware as well as software systems and try to take up projects at their intersection. You can check out my academic CV for more information.

During my undergraduate studies, I was the President of the Electronics and Instrumentation Association of my campus. Along with my team, I started a series of short, interactive tech-sessions, The Student Forum BPGC, where students and professionals, with a background in research, shared their experience with interested students. These sessions are active presently as well. I also help maintain the BITS R&D blog which is updated regularly with articles about BITSians’ contributions in research and development activities.

I was an integral member of the Department of Photography, BITS Goa where I was involved in event photography, but soon shifted my focus to videography and video editing. I also worked with Dr. Rayson K. Alex on various videos and faculty interviews. I enjoy video and film editing projects. Contact me by email if you have a freelance project for me in this domain.
