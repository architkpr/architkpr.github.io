About

My name is Archit Kapoor and I am a graduate student at Columbia University, New York. Read on to find out about my career interests and experience.

I am passionate about the Internet of Things (IoT), sensor networks and network security. I aim to contribute to improving the security and privacy of IoT devices and networks.

I graduated in 2019 with a B.E. (Hons.) in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering from BITS Pilani, India. I pursued my undergraduate thesis at the University of New South Wales, Australia. The topic of my thesis was ‘Securing the Internet of Things using a Lightweight Scalable Blockchain’.

In 2019, I also completed a summer internship at Ernst & Young, one of the largest consulting firms, during which I was a member of the Cybersecurity team. I assisted in the security testing and reporting of web applications.

In the summer of 2020, I interned with Silicon Labs, a leading IoT solutions provider, as a Software Engineer on the Software QA team, working on security testing.

I will be graduating this December, with a master’s in Computer Engineering, specializing in cybersecurity, networking and IoT. I am actively looking for full-time roles in these industries. Feel free to reach out to me on LinkedIn, if you’d like to connect with me.
